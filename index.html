Our Story

Care Built on
Warmth & Trust

Sallies Warm HealthCare was founded on one simple belief — every person deserves to age or recover at home with grace, comfort, and the support they need. We are a non-medical private duty agency based in Houston, TX, dedicated to enhancing the quality of life for our clients.

Led by owner Sarah Abu, our caregivers treat every client like family. Whether it's help with daily routines, household tasks, or getting to appointments, we show up — warm, reliable, and ready.
